Ingredients

  • Lean meat: Sausage and ground beef
  • Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese needed: Parmesan, Mozzerlla, ricotta
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
